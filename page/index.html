Surrealism

Surrealism is an early 20th-century art movement that explores the unconscious mind through dreamlike and bizarre imagery. Artists like Salvador Dalí and René Magritte used unexpected contrasts to challenge logic, blending fantasy and reality in thought-provoking ways.

Decoration

Decoration is the art of enhancing a space’s aesthetics and functionality through carefully chosen elements like furniture, colors, textures, and accessories. It aims to create a balanced, pleasing environment that reflects personal style while optimizing comfort and usability.

Futuristic

Futuristic design emphasizes advanced technology, sleek aesthetics, and innovative concepts that envision a world of tomorrow. It incorporates bold shapes, metallic finishes, and minimalistic details, aiming to create an environment that feels progressive and cutting-edge.
